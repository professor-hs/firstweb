About Health Club

Talent wins games, but teamwork and hard work win championships. We're the Health Club at VIT. Our mission is to support students' well-being and happiness by organizing health and wellness workshops and events. Whether it's physical fitness, mental health, or emotional well-being, we're here to create a supportive space where everyone can thrive.
